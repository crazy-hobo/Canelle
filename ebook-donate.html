Cannelle La Demoiselle

Inspired by a true story. Once upon a time, there was a little French girl, Cannelle la demoiselle . . .

Our heroine, Cannelle, is 11 years old (the same age as the author’s daughter.) The author started the book when her daughter was 4 years old. Cannelle is French, during six years, she was prevented from going back to France. However, Cannelle can dream, and her dream becomes an adventure when she shares the tale of her journey to her land, France.

Cannelle will visit a new country each year with her mother, who is a journalist and wants to share her passion for writing and traveling with her daughter. In this first book, Cannelle goes to France, where you and she will visit famous places in Paris. The writing is peppered with wellknown French expressions and idioms, giving it Cannelle’s own style. The second book will be set in the San Francisco Bay Area, where an older Cannelle lives with her friends, three special animals: a pelican who thinks he is a dog, a soft lizard, and a raccoon who is afraid of nothing. Let us not forget Cannelle’s other friends at L’Ecole Bilingue of Berkeley, where the author’s daughter went to school for seven years.

Thanks to our kind readers for their patience, and now let’s follow the marvelous adventures of Little Miss Cannelle and Kandy the Kitten!

Little Miss Cannelle In Paris, France.

1. Meeting Kandy the Kitten

Cannelle is a brave, and determined little girl. It is her first day in Paris. While walking in Le Marais, she suddenly notices a small black and white ball beneath a distant bench. Cannelle goes up to this little squirming shape and is amazed to discover it is a kitten! What a surprise! As she loves cats, she caresses it and speaks reassuringly to it. « This little cat must be about two months old » says her mother. Cannelle begs her to let the kitten stay with them, at least during their time in Paris. Turning red with temper, Cannelle doesn’t take long to convince her mother -- who would be ready to bring even the moon to her. So it is decided, Cannelle gets to keep the cat. She names it « Kandy the Kitten ». Happy with its newfound family, the kitten mews and purrs loudly. Kandy is special. His two unique features are quite long whiskers, and mismatched eyes, one of is turquoise and the other bright green, just like Cannelle’s emerald green eyes.

Cannelle la demoiselle A Paris, France.

Rencontre avec Bonbon le chaton Cannelle est une petite fille courageuse et determinee. C‘est son premier jour à Paris. Soudain, elle voit au loin une petite boule beige et noire qui bouge sous un banc. Cannelle fonce vers cette tache qui se deplace et decouvre avec surprise un chaton ! Surprenante decouverte ! Comme elle adore les chats, elle le caresse pour le rassurer et lui parle. "Un siamois d'environ 2 mois" dit maman. Cannelle implore sa maman de le garder, un temps seulement, le temps de leur sejour à Paris. «Etre verte de rage»* Cannelle sait bien y faire avec maman ! Maman irait volontiers «decrocher la lune»* pour sa fille. Alors c’est decidé, Cannelle le garde et le nomme «bonbon le chaton». Heureux de sa nouvelle famille, le chaton remercie avec des miaulements de satisfaction et ronronne bruyamment. Bonbon le chaton est unique ! Voici ses 2 particularites : il a de longues moustaches et un oeil bleu turquoise et l’autre vert emeraude comme les yeux de Cannelle, vert emeraude.
